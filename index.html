About Me

I am Meenu Sharma. I have completed my BSc (Non-Medical with Computer Science) from Pt NRS Govt College Rohtak.

Recently earned my Master of Computer Science from Maharshi Dayanand University Rohtak.

During my MSc, I worked on a project focused on developing an MCQ test management system using Python. Currently, I am working on an MCQ test management system using the Streamlit library. This project has provided me with a deep understanding of software development processes, from initial design through to implementation and testing.

I have a diverse skill set that includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, along with hands-on experience in Python and JavaScript. My passion lies in web development, especially in front-end development, where I enjoy creating intuitive and user-friendly interfaces.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have actively engaged in various workshops and conferences to broaden my knowledge and network within the industry. I participated in a workshop organized by Tech Mahindra Nandi Foundation at Maharshi Dayanand University, which provided insights into industry practices and emerging technologies. I also attended a national conference on future computing technologies for sustainable development, held in collaboration with Chaudhry Ranbir Singh Institute of Social & Economic Change, where I learned about the latest trends and innovations in computing.

I am highly motivated and eager to apply my skills and knowledge to real-world challenges. I am constantly seeking opportunities to learn and grow, aiming to make a meaningful impact in the field of web development.
